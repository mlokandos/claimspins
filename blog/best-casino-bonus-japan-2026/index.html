What is a wagering requirement and how does it affect my bonus?
A wagering requirement (also called a playthrough requirement) specifies how many times you must bet the bonus amount before you can withdraw any winnings derived from it. For example, a €100 bonus with a 35x wagering requirement means you must place a total of €3,500 in bets before the resulting winnings become withdrawable. Lower wagering requirements — like SpinIt's 30x — are generally more achievable for most players.
